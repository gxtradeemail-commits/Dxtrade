FAQ

Common
questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I deposit funds into DXTRADE? +

Send Nigerian Naira (NGN) via bank transfer to our Opay account. Once sent, open the app, go to Deposit, enter the NGN amount you transferred and your transaction reference number. Your balance is credited in USD at the rate of ₦1,470 = $1 within 15–30 minutes after admin approval.

How are withdrawals processed? +

Withdrawals are sent directly to your Nigerian bank account in NGN. The rate is $1 = ₦1,360. Minimum withdrawal is $1.00. You'll provide your bank name, account number and account name. Admin processes withdrawals within 24 hours.

What happens when my bot plan ends? +

When your bot plan completes its duration (10, 20 or 30 days), your original capital is automatically returned to your account balance along with all accumulated daily ROI. You can then reinvest or withdraw immediately.

How does the referral program work? +

Every user has a unique referral code found in the Referral section of the app. When someone signs up using your code, both you and the new user receive a $0.41 (equivalent to ₦600 at deposit rate) welcome bonus instantly credited to your accounts.

Is there a minimum deposit amount? +

There is no platform minimum deposit — you can deposit any amount in NGN. However, each bot plan has its own minimum investment: Bot Alpha starts at $2.04 (≈₦3,000), Bot Sigma at $17.01 (≈₦25,000), and Bot Omega at $102.04 (≈₦150,000).

Can I run multiple bots at the same time? +

Yes. You can activate multiple bot plans simultaneously as long as you have sufficient balance. The dashboard shows all running bots with individual progress bars and earned totals. Each bot runs independently.
